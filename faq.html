Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer at Ammeerullahi & Brothers Autos?
We provide general repairs, diagnostics, wheel balancing and alignment, electrical services, spray painting, car wash and sales of lubricants, tyres, brake pads, brake disc, stabilizer linkage, coolant, ATF and accessories.
Where are your workshops located?
We have two workshops:
1. Shafa Filling Station, after Dunamis Church, Sabo Lugbe, FCT Abuja
2. NNPC Filling Station, opposite Government Day Secondary School, Bank Road, Gwagwalada, FCT Abuja.
How can I book a service?
You can book a service by clicking the "Book Service" button on our website, calling us directly, or visiting any of our workshops.
Do you offer emergency roadside assistance?
Yes, we provide emergency roadside assistance within Abuja. Contact us immediately for help.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfers, and POS payments at our workshops.
Can I get genuine spare parts from your workshop?
Absolutely! We deal only in genuine and high-quality spare parts to ensure the durability of your vehicle.
