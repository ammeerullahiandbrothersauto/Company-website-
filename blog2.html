Top 7 Tips for Maintaining Your Vehicle

Keeping your car in top condition doesn’t have to be difficult. With simple steps, you can extend its life, improve safety, and save money. Here are 7 practical tips:

1. Change Oil Regularly

Follow the manufacturer’s schedule to keep your engine healthy.

2. Check Tyre Pressure

Proper inflation improves fuel efficiency and reduces wear.

3. Replace Air Filters

Clean filters improve engine performance and air quality inside the car.

4. Inspect Brakes

Listen for squealing and check pads often to avoid safety risks.

5. Keep Fluids Topped Up

Coolant, transmission, brake fluid — all are essential to smooth operation.

6. Wash & Wax

Protects paintwork, prevents rust, and maintains resale value.

7. Schedule Regular Checkups

A professional service ensures hidden issues are caught early.

Final Tip: Consistency is key. Regular maintenance saves you from expensive repairs.
